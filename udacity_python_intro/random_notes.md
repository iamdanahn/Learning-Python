### String method
.format()

```py
print("Mohammed has {} balloons".format(27))
# Mohammed has 27 balloons

animal = "dog"
action = "bite"
print("Does your {} {}?".format(animal, action))
# Does your dog bite?

maria_string = "Maria loves {} and {}"
print(maria_string.format("math", "statistics"))
# Maria loves math and statistics
```
